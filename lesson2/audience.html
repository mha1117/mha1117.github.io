City Weather

Your weather update all day!



Target Audience


The target audience is anyone who is looking to find out about the weather. It can be used by anyone who has the internet connection. It can be used by people who are booking holidays to check the weather in different country. It is also used by anyone who wants to go on a fun day out.

Personas

Joshua

Joshua's work is in marketing department. He is responsible in making marketing strategy and visiting other school to introduce our institution.

Rosalie

Rosalie loves an outdoor activity like camping and hiking. She always use weather website to check the weather condition to her target place.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Joshua used the City Weather website to check the weather if it is possible for him to used his motorcycle to travel going to main branch of his school.

Scenario 2

Rosalie is planning to go on a hike. She choose to go in pinagbanderahan, but when she check a weather forecast in city weather she learns that its going to rain, so she postponed her plan.

Scenario 3

Joshua is set to speak at career guidance at SDNHS, as he checked the weather condition he learns that its going to be a sunny day, so he choose to used his motorcyle to travel.

Scenario 4

One of the marketing strategy that Joshua plans is to give flyers in a public places, as he begun to plan, he make sure to checked the weather to know when is the perfect time and day to gives flyers.

Scenario 5

Rosalie plans to invite Joshua and her friends to a vacation trip. She check the weather forecast to make sure that their itenerary is suited for the weather situation.
